Simplified workflow

Agile workflow

To simplify your workflow, EazyTrucker provides you with a lovely SCRUM task board that helps focus and prioritize all transactions into 3 pipelines (NEW JOBS, IN PROGRESS and COMPLETED), ensuring that important notifications are always bubbled to the work items in real time to give you situational awareness. The work items display current states through color tags and provide quick access to the next action.

Information security

Security

EazyTrucker incorporates a role based design which ensures access constraints on the users based on assigned roles (e.g. driver allowed to receive alerts but not view bids). Transactional information are also kept safe by implementing high level security standards on our software and servers.

Simple deal-based pricing.

Eazytrucker does not use time-based fees, in which your credit card is debited regardless of whether you are generating revenue or not. With Eazytrucker, you only pay for successful transactions i.e. when you accept a bid. You can choose the default Pay-As-You-Go scheme in which you pay by credit card for every successful transaction or, take advantage of the generous discounts offered by our pre-payment plan.
