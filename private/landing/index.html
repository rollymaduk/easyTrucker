Agile workflow

To simplify your workflow, EazyTrucker provides you with a lovely SCRUM task board that helps focus and prioritize all transactions into 3 pipelines (NEW JOBS, IN PROGRESS and COMPLETED), ensuring that important notifications are always bubbled to the work items in real time to give you situational awareness.
